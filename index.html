About This Map
Overview

This interactive map shows proposed and historical logging activity surrounding the Tsitka and, Adam and Eve watersheds.

Included Layers

RED stripped polygons indicate areas where logging is proposed to take place. Old Growth Management Areas are shown using BLACK horizontal lines. These are areas where logging is prevented or restrained. WHITE dotted polygons are areas that the Technical Advisory Panel (TAP) has identified as Priority Deferral Areas. BROWN polygons show small (lighter) to large (darker) old growth stands. The YELLOW previously logged layer shows polygons from the Consolidated Cutblocks dataset, and carefully selected VRI data under 100 years of age. Critical Habitat for federally listed species at risk is identified in BLUE. For more information click on your polygon of interest and toggle layers on and off using the legend.
